Introduction

TokenVisor Enterprise is the access and control plane for enterprise AI. It sits between users, applications, and model endpoints, providing a single governed entry point to approved external LLM providers or self-hosted inference managed by TokenVisor. With policy-based routing, identity-aware access, audit logging, and budget controls, TokenVisor helps organisations standardise how AI is consumed across teams without sacrificing flexibility.

Instead of forcing users through slow IT tickets or pushing them toward unmanaged tools, TokenVisor provides a self-service portal where teams can request and obtain AI API keys under project-level guardrails. IT and project owners can enforce rate limits, spend limits, model policies, and safety controls, while every request remains tracked and attributable. The result is faster adoption, lower shadow AI risk, and a governed foundation for production AI.
