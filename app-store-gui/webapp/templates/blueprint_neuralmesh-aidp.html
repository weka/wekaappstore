Introduction

The WEKA AI Data Platform (AIDP) is a software and hardware add-on to WEKA NeuralMesh™ that transforms how enterprises operationalize their AI factories. Rather than building and maintaining custom data preparation pipelines, AIDP automatically processes data as it lands on NeuralMesh, keeping it continuously AI-ready as files are created, updated, or deleted. AIDP makes data immediately available to any customer AI pipeline that needs it and delivers ready-to-consume AI outcomes such as semantic search without requiring organizations to manage AI frameworks themselves.

AIDP closes the gap between storing data on NeuralMesh and using that data in AI projects. It is designed for enterprise organizations that would like to start with their AI Factory or simply consume AI as an outcome.
